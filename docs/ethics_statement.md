# Ethics Statement

## Research Ethics

This research was conducted following established principles of responsible security research and academic ethics.

### Authorization and Approval

- **Scanning Scope**: All scans were performed on authorized network infrastructure (CERNET2)
- **Institutional Approval**: Research conducted with appropriate institutional permissions
- **Compliance**: Full compliance with CERNET2 usage policies and regulations

### Data Collection Principles

#### Minimization
We collected only the minimum data necessary for our research objectives:
- Network port exposure status
- Service version information (publicly available)
- Port distribution statistics

#### Privacy Protection
- **IP Addresses**: Partially anonymized in all publications and datasets
- **No Personal Data**: No personal information was collected or stored
- **No Traffic Content**: Only connection metadata was analyzed
- **No Authentication Data**: No credentials or authentication tokens were accessed

### Responsible Disclosure

#### Vulnerabilities
Identified vulnerabilities were handled responsibly:
- No exploitation of vulnerabilities beyond demonstration
- Vulnerabilities reported through appropriate channels
- Detailed findings shared only in anonymized form

#### Service Access
- No unauthorized access to any system
- No modification of any system
- No extraction of any private data
- No disruption of any service

### Harm Mitigation

#### Network Impact
- Scanning rate limited to prevent network congestion
- Scanning performed during off-peak hours when possible
- Bandwidth consumption monitored and controlled

#### Service Impact
- No attempts to crash or degrade services
- Connection timeouts strictly enforced
- No repeated connection attempts to same host

### Transparency

#### Methodology Disclosure
Full methodology documented for reproducibility:
- Scan parameters and configurations
- Data collection procedures
- Analysis methods

#### Limitations Acknowledged
- Honeypot networks identified and excluded from analysis
- Measurement errors acknowledged
- Data quality limitations documented

### Academic Ethics

#### Citation
All prior work properly cited and attributed.

#### Authorship
All contributors properly acknowledged.

#### Conflicts of Interest
No conflicts of interest to declare.

### Legal Compliance

This research complies with:
- CERNET2 usage policies
- Chinese cybersecurity regulations
- International research ethics standards
- Academic integrity requirements

This research was reviewed and approved by the relevant institutional ethics committee.
